What is 5G Technology?

5G is the 5th generation mobile network. It is a new global wireless standard after 1G, 2G, 3G, and 4G networks. 5G enables a new kind of network that is designed to connect virtually everyone and everything together including machines, objects, and devices.

With breakthrough speeds, minimal lag, and massive capacity, 5G is set to revolutionize industries from healthcare to manufacturing, entertainment to transportation. It's not just an upgrade—it's a transformation in how we connect.

NEXA's 5G network utilizes cutting-edge technology including massive MIMO, beamforming, and millimeter wave spectrum to deliver unprecedented performance and reliability across urban centers and expanding into suburban and rural areas.
