Frequently Asked Questions

How do I switch to Nexa?

expand_more

Switching to Nexa is simple! Just choose a plan that fits your needs, and we'll help you transfer your number from your current provider. The process typically takes 1-2 business days to complete.

Can I keep my current phone number?

expand_more

Yes, absolutely! We make it easy to bring your current phone number to Nexa. During the signup process, you'll have the option to port your existing number. There's no additional cost for this service.

What is 5G coverage like?

expand_more

Nexa offers extensive 5G coverage across the country, with continuous expansion. Our 5G network provides faster speeds and lower latency. Check our coverage map for specific details about your area.

How can I track my data usage?

expand_more

You can easily track your data usage through our mobile app or customer portal. We also send notifications when you're approaching your data limit to help you avoid overage charges.

What payment methods do you accept?

expand_more

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. You can also set up automatic payments for convenience and never worry about missing a payment.
