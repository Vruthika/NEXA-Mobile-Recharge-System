Frequently Asked Questions

How do I recharge my mobile?

To recharge your mobile, simply log in to your NEXA account, select the "Recharge" option from the dashboard, enter your mobile number, choose your preferred plan or enter a custom amount, select your payment method, and proceed to checkout. Once payment is successful, your mobile will be recharged instantly with SMS confirmation.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept a wide range of payment methods including all major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), net banking from 100+ banks, UPI payments (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), mobile wallets, and digital payment platforms. All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption for your safety.

How can I check my recharge history?

You can easily check your complete recharge history by logging into your NEXA account and navigating to "My Account" > "Recharge History". Here you'll find detailed information about all your past transactions including date, amount, mobile number, transaction ID, and status. You can also download receipts and filter by date range.

What should I do if my recharge fails?

If your recharge fails, don't worry! First, check your "Recharge History" to confirm the transaction status. If the amount has been debited but recharge wasn't processed, it will be automatically refunded within 5-7 business days. For immediate assistance, contact our 24/7 support team with your transaction ID. We also provide instant retry options for failed transactions.

How do I change my registered mobile number?

To update your registered mobile number, go to "My Account" > "Profile Settings" and click on "Edit Mobile Number". Enter your new mobile number and click "Update". You'll receive an OTP on the new number for verification. Once verified, your account will be updated with the new mobile number. This ensures security and helps us send you important transaction updates.

Are there any additional charges for recharges?

NEXA offers transparent pricing with no hidden charges! We don't charge any convenience fees for standard recharges. The amount you pay is exactly what goes to your mobile account. However, some premium payment methods might have minimal processing fees which are clearly displayed before payment confirmation.
