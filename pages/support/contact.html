Frequently Asked Questions

How do I recharge my mobile?

To recharge your mobile, log in to your account, select the "Recharge" option, enter your mobile number, choose a plan or enter an amount, and proceed to payment. Once payment is successful, your mobile will be recharged instantly.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, mobile wallets, and more. All transactions are secure and encrypted for your safety.

How can I check my recharge history?

You can check your recharge history by logging into your account and navigating to "My Account" > "Recharge History". Here you'll find details of all your past transactions.

What should I do if my recharge fails?

If your recharge fails, don't worry. The amount will be refunded to your payment source within 5-7 business days. You can also check the status in your "Recharge History" or contact our support team for assistance.

How do I change my registered mobile number?

To change your registered mobile number, go to "My Account" > "Profile Settings" and update your mobile number. You'll receive a verification code on the new number to confirm the change.
